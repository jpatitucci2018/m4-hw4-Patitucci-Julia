Alec Benjamin

Background

Alec Benjamin is a 29 year old singer, songwriter, and musician. He is originaly from Phoenix, Arizona and had his breakthrough in 2018 with his single "Let me doen slowly". He won a grammy in 2017 for his collaboration with Alessia Cara.
